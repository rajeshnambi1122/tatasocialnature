About The Marathon

The event aims to raise awareness among the people about our duty to protect the Thamirabarani River, which serves as a lifeline for the people of Tirunelveli. How to Register Online?

âœ¨ Glimpse of Tirunelveli

Tirunelveli, known as the "City of Paddy Fields" and famous for its unique Halwa sweet, is one of the oldest cities in Tamil Nadu. It is situated on the eastern banks of the perennial Thamirabarani River, which serves as the lifeline for the people of this region.

Home to numerous ancient temples including the Nellaiappar Temple, historic monuments, and serene natural landscapes, Tirunelveli offers a perfect blend of cultural heritage and natural beauty.

The city is also known for its significant contribution to education, with several renowned educational institutions.

Our marathon celebrates not only environmental awareness but also showcases the rich heritage and beauty of this historic city.
